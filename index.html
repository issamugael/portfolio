About Me

Hi, i'm Gabriel! But you can call me Gael. I was born in Aichi, Japan, but currently live in Brazil. Since i was a child, my biggest passion was to create stuff: game concepts, toys, drawings, etc. And fortunately was able to pursue this as a path for my life.Most of my recent work consists of illustrations and graphic designs, such as shirt designs and posters.
